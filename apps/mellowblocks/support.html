Support

For Mellow Blocks — iOS & Android

Mellow Blocks is in final polish for launch. The fastest way to reach us — for early feedback, questions, or bug reports — is hello@bemolabs.com. We read every message and reply within two business days, usually sooner.

What to include in a bug report

  • Device model + OS version (iOS or Android)
  • App version (visible in the app's Settings screen)
  • What you were doing when it happened
  • A screenshot or screen recording if you can
